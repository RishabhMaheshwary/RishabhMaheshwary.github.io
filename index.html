I am Rishabh Maheshwary, currently an AI Resident at Meta/Facebook AI Research. My research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Multimodal understanding of vision and language content.

At Facebook, I am working with Marcus Rohrbach on tasks involving multimodal understanding of images and text. Before starting the residency, I completed my Masters from IIIT Hyderabad, India where my work focused on evaluating the robustness and generalization of NLP models. Previously, I was also a research intern at Verisk AI where I worked on the task of information extraction from form-like documents. Apart from this, I also did Google Summer of Code internship and I am an active contributor to several open-source frameworks.
